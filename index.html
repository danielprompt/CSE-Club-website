Our Purpose

The Computer Science & Engineering Club was founded to bring together students who love building, designing, and innovating with technology. We meet in Room C135 every Monday and Wednesday from 12:30 PM to 2:00 PM, where ideas quickly turn into projects. From programming with Arduino and Raspberry Pi to developing our own Unity-based game, every meeting is about learning by doing. Our purpose is to create an open, collaborative space for students of all experience levels to explore computer science and engineering beyond the classroom.
