Our Mission and Story

We started this club to bring tech-savvy students together and make cool stuff happen. Our mission is simple: build fun projects, learn by doing, and support each other as we grow our skills. Whether it’s coding, circuits, or crazy ideas, we’re all about hands-on learning and creativity.

Projects

Rasberry Pi and Object Detection

This project uses a Raspberry Pi, AI camera module, and YOLOv8 to detect and count people in real-time. The live video feed is streamed securely over the internet and can be controlled remotely.

View Project

Arduino Car

An Arduino-powered Bluetooth car that responds to joystick input from a mobile app. It features directional control, custom speed logic, and additional features like horn via button commands

About Us

We are a community of students passionate about coding, robotics, and problem-solving. Our club provides a space to build real-world skills through team-based projects, workshops, and competitions. Whether you're a beginner or experienced, we welcome anyone curious about the world of computer science and engineering.
