About Us

Innovate. Create. Collaborate.

The Computer Science & Engineering Club is a hub for students who love to build and explore technology.

We meet in Room C135 every Monday and Wednesday from 12:30 PM to 2:00 PM. Whether you're into coding, robotics, game dev, or AI, this is the place to turn ideas into reality. We host workshops, build collaborative projects, and support each other in learning skills that go beyond the classroom.
